Who We Are

About Us

We are an independent insurance brokerage dedicated to providing reliable, straightforward cover for individuals, families, and businesses across Ireland. With access to a wide panel of leading insurers, we offer tailored solutions for all your insurance needs.

Our approach is simple: professional advice, competitive options, and service you can depend on. We take the time to understand each client's needs so we can recommend the right level of protection — not just the cheapest policy, but the most suitable one.

Whether you are insuring your home, protecting your business, or managing farm risks, we aim to make the process clear and efficient. As an independent broker, we work on your behalf, comparing policies and negotiating with insurers to secure appropriate cover at a fair price.

We pride ourselves on building long-term relationships with our clients through honest guidance, responsive support, and ongoing policy reviews as your needs change. From first quote to renewal, we are here to provide dependable insurance advice you can trust.

Parking Nearby

There are a number of convenient parking options within easy walking distance. Public car parks close by include City Park @ Market Square, Parnell Street Car Park, and Lower Market Street Carpark, as well as Bindon Street Carpark and Cornmarket Street Carpark. There is also convenient parking at Abbey Street Car Park and Dunnes Stores Customer Car Park for short visits.

Ennis operates a Pay & Display system for most public car parks and on-street spaces, with pay-by-phone and app payment options available across the town centre. Charges and durations vary by location and time of day, so please check signage on arrival. If you need guidance on the best place to park, just give us a call and we'll be happy to help.
